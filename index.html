About Me

Experienced full-stack developer proficient in JavaScript, React, Ruby, and Rails. With a professional background in public education, I am always helping people effectively solve problems. From a previous career as a band director, I am highly self-motivated, used to managing large groups of people at one time, and can very quickly adapt to any work environment. As a band director and educator, I wore many different hats, and I take pride in my ability to pick up new skills quickly, work well with others and I am not afraid to seek help from my colleagues. I got into the tech field because I wanted to extend my reach to help others. My goal is to combine my skills as an educator as well as my technical skills to help companies provide a tailored-fit experience that will make their clients feel heard and connected with the company.
